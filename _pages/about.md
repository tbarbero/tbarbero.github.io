Currently, I'm a fourth year undergraduate at Scripps Institution of Oceanograpy studying Oceanic and Atmospheric Science with a minor in mathematics. I work in the Evan Research Lab researching dust storm meteorlogy. I am utilizing a GNSS receiver to retrieve precipitable water vapor. This quantity helps us to improve our understanding of the underlying physics of dust storms within the Salton Basin. In my leisure time, you might find me climbing obsessively, listening to a podcast, or kickin' it with friends.
