Hi there! I'm Tyler, a fourth year undergraduate at Scripps Institution of Oceanography, UCSD, studying Oceanic and Atmospheric Sciences. Since the summer of 2019, I've been working in the Evan Research Lab. One of our research topics is dust storm meteorology based in the Salton Sea in Southern California. Part of my role encompasses using GPS to retrieve Precipitable Water Vapor. This is one of many quantities that will help us understand underlying physics of dust storms in this region.
